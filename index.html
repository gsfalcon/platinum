This page is a collection of all my platinum trophies for PlayStation. I have been collecting trophies since the PlayStation 3 era and have amassed a decent collection over the years. I am proud of my accomplishments and I enjoy sharing them with others.

One of the new features of the PlayStation 5 is the ability to save the last 30 seconds of gameplay before a trophy pops. I have been trying to save these videos for as many of my platinum trophies as possible. I have included links to these videos below each trophy. I will continue to add videos as I earn more platinums.

List of Platinum Trophies

Assassin's Creed Origins PS4

The platinum trophy in Assassin's Creed Origins is relatively straightforward, offering a manageable challenge for completionists. While there are some missable elements, overall, it's quite achievable. One notable trophy requires selling various trinkets at once, adding a layer of strategy to inventory management.

After that, it's largely about exploration and completion. Clearing the map, uncovering the mysteries of Egypt's pyramids and tombs, and engaging with the game's rich world are key components of the journey towards platinum.

Personally, I'd rate the platinum journey a 7 out of 10. This score reflects my subjective enjoyment, immersion, and overall experience rather than an official rating.
